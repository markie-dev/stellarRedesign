Location:

The Arbors subdivision offers an ideal setting for country living within a small town setting. Only a few minutes south of the VA and Scott and White hospitals, this quiet family oriented community features serene nature setting with plenty of nature and tall hardwood trees, while still offering all the comforts of city services. Located just west of the high school on FM 436, THE ARBORS gives the nature lover reason to be part of this sleepy community. Why not get out of the proverbial rat-race and be part of some peaceful living?


Features:

The homes in The Arbors subdivision are rich-feeling with large back yards, tall ceilings, and elegant living areas. Great care is given to all facets of the construction process including the meticulous design of the foundation, framing, plumbing and other non-visible areas of the home. While many homebuilders have switched to cheaper construction methods, Stellar has chosen to maintain its quality position with such items as high efficiency polyurethane insulation (icynene) in attics and walls, extended warranty Termidor termite barrier FULL pre-treat, slip sheet protection for tile areas, 16" center studs and joists. Each home will be further accented by elegant fixtures and faucets and high grade flooring in a color-coordinated fashion.
