Features:

The homes in Highland Park Estates subdivision are rich-feeling with large back yards, tall ceilings, and elegant living areas. Great care is given to all facets of the construction process including the meticulous design of the foundation, framing, plumbing and other non-visible areas of the home. While many homebuilders have switched to cheaper construction methods, Stellar has chosen to maintain its quality position with such items as high efficiency polyurethane insulation (icynene) in attics and walls, extended warranty Termidor termite barrier FULL pre-treat, slip sheet protection for tile areas, 16" center studs and joists. Each home will be further accented by elegant fixtures and faucets and high grade flooring in a color-coordinated fashion.
